Community News Bulletin

Local City Council Announces Green Initiative Funding

The City Council is thrilled to announce a new grant cycle targeting sustainable community projects. Over $150,000 has been allocated for tree planting, community gardens, solar installations, and educational programs on ecology. Residents are encouraged to apply before August 15th to be considered.

Workshops will be held at the library every Saturday to assist community organizers with drafting their applications. A panel of environmental scientists will provide direct feedback. We hope to see a wide range of creative and green-conscious project submissions.
