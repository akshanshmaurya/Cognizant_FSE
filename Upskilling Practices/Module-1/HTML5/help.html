Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I register for an event?

A: Go to the main page, navigate to the "Register" section, fill in your details, select your preferred event category, and click the "Submit Registration" button.

Q: What are the registration fees?

A: Fees vary by category:

  • Tech Meetups: $15.00
  • Sports Tournaments: $10.00
  • Arts and Crafts: $20.00
  • Charity Runs: Free

Q: How does the geolocation feature work?

A: When you click "Find Nearby Events", the browser requests access to your location. Once granted, it determines your exact latitude and longitude to show nearby events. Your privacy is protected.

Q: How can I change my event selection preferences?

A: The form automatically saves your selection to local storage. If you want to reset or change it, you can select another category or click "Clear Preferences" to delete saved values.
